# Operational Responsibility Layers (Draft v0.1)

This document clarifies the separation of responsibility across the Author's
operational identities. These layers are conceptual interfaces and do not
represent legal entities.

## 1. ari-kun (Lightweight Layer)
- Casual communication
- Low-risk tasks
- Experimental or learning activities
- No binding commitments
- No impact on upper layers

## 2. noikunya-dev (Developer Layer)
- GitHub development activities
- Code implementation and review
- Technical discussions
- Prototype and draft work
- Independent from ari-kun actions

## 3. chief (Technical Responsibility Layer)
- GitLab operations
- License management
- OSS release responsibility
- Technical decision-making
- Independent from lower layers

## 4. CEO (Executive Layer)
- Commercial negotiations
- Banking and legal interactions
- Final decision authority
- Only invoked for high-stakes matters

## Separation Principle
Actions or statements made under one layer MUST NOT be interpreted as
commitments or liabilities of any other layer. This ensures the Author's
core identity remains protected and unaffected by operational context.
